class = kitchen
Attributes:
  - food(hash)
  - clean (boolean)
  - amount of cooks (integer)
  - size (float)

Methods:
  - cooking food would decrease hash values according to what was used.
  - cleaning at the end of the day would result in true for boolean type.
  - hiring new cook results in increase of integer attribute.
  - remodeling kitchen would change size and increase or decrease kitchen float value.
